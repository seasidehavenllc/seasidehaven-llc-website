Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our services, scheduling, quotes, and more.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Lynchburg, Roanoke, Bedford, Smith Mountain Lake, and surrounding areas.

How fast can I get a quote?

Most quotes are returned same‑day or within 24 hours depending on service type.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes — limited emergency availability for urgent repairs, property issues, and safety concerns.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card, digital payments, and business invoicing depending on the service.

Do you require deposits?

Some services require a small deposit, especially larger projects or scheduled work.

Can I schedule online?

Yes — use our Contact or Get a Quote page to schedule services quickly.

Are your services insured?

Yes — Seaside Haven LLC is fully insured for all services we provide.

Do you offer recurring service plans?

Yes — landscaping, pressure washing, and property maintenance can be scheduled weekly or monthly.
