All Licenses & Certificates
Purpose

This document consolidates all official licenses and certificates that Seaside Haven LLC can obtain for free or low cost. These documents support legal compliance across all active and future divisions.

EIN Confirmation Letter (FREE)

Issued by the IRS upon business registration.
• Source: Internal Revenue Service
• Cost: $0
• Required For: All divisions

Document Location: EIN-Confirmation-Letter.pdf

DOT Number (FREE)

Required for commercial hauling or interstate transport.
• Source: FMCSA
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Junk Removal, Landscaping, Pressure Washing

Document Location: DOT-Number-Confirmation.pdf

Waste Disposal Permit (FREE)

Required for dumping at county landfills or transfer stations.
• Source: Local County Waste Authority
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Junk Removal, Landscaping

Document Location: Waste-Disposal-Permit.pdf

Short‑Term Rental Tax Registration (FREE)

Required for Airbnb or STR operations.
• Source: Local Commissioner of Revenue
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Airbnb / STR Division

Document Location: STR-Tax-Registration.pdf

Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (FREE)

Required for STR properties and some commercial spaces.
• Source: Local Fire Marshal
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Airbnb / STR Division

Document Location: Fire-Safety-Inspection.pdf

Tourism / Lodging Tax Registration (FREE)

Required for STR and lodging operations.
• Source: Local Commissioner of Revenue
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Airbnb / STR Division

Document Location: Tourism-Tax-Registration.pdf

Wastewater Runoff Compliance (FREE)

Required for pressure washing operations.
• Source: Local Environmental Office
• Cost: $0
• Required For: Pressure Washing Division

Document Location: Wastewater-Compliance.pdf

Vendor License (FREE or Low Cost)

Required for event‑based sales or live commerce.
• Source: Local County Clerk
• Cost: $0–$25
• Required For: Live Commerce Partner

Document Location: Vendor-License.pdf

Commercial Vehicle Use Certificate ($20–$50)

• Required For: Junk Removal, Landscaping, Pressure Washing

Document Location: Commercial-Vehicle-Certificate.pdf

County Business License ($20–$50)

• Source: Local Commissioner of Revenue
• Cost: $20–$50
• Required For: All divisions

Document Location: County-Business-License.pdf

Vending Machine Permit ($10–$50)

Required for vending machine operations.
• Source: Local County Clerk
• Cost: $10–$50
• Required For: Vending Machine Division

Document Location: Vending-Machine-Permit.pdf

Short‑Term Rental Permit ($50–$100)

• Required For: Airbnb / STR Division

Document Location: STR-Permit.pdf
