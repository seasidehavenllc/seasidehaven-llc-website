Daily Operations Checklist
Purpose

This checklist ensures consistent daily operations across all divisions of Seaside Haven LLC. It covers opening tasks, workflow readiness, job execution, communication standards, and closing procedures.

Opening Tasks

• Review schedule for the day
• Confirm team assignments
• Check for urgent messages or updates
• Inspect vehicles (fuel, loadout, cleanliness)
• Verify tools and equipment availability
• Prepare materials for scheduled jobs
• Ensure PPE is stocked and available

Pre‑Job Preparation

• Review job notes and customer details
• Confirm addresses and travel routes
• Contact customer if clarification is needed
• Load required tools and materials
• Verify weather conditions (if relevant)
• Ensure all team members understand the plan

During Job Execution

• Arrive on time
• Greet customer professionally
• Walk through job scope
• Identify hazards or special considerations
• Protect customer property
• Maintain clean and safe work areas
• Document progress with photos
• Communicate updates when appropriate

Customer Communication

• Provide clear, polite updates
• Avoid discussing pricing unless authorized
• Direct concerns to management
• Maintain professional tone at all times
• Confirm satisfaction before leaving site

Job Completion

• Clean and organize job site
• Verify work meets quality standards
• Take final photos
• Complete digital job report
• Log any issues or follow‑up needs
• Close job in system

End‑of‑Day Closing Tasks

• Return tools and equipment to proper storage
• Clean vehicles and remove debris
• Restock materials and PPE
• Review completed jobs
• Log any incidents or notes
• Prepare for next day’s schedule
• Secure all facilities and vehicles
