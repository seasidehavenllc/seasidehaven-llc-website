Customer Service Playbook
Purpose

This playbook outlines the communication standards, customer interaction expectations, and service principles that guide every division of Seaside Haven LLC. Consistent customer service builds trust, strengthens our brand, and ensures a positive experience from first contact to job completion.

Core Customer Service Principles

• Professionalism at all times
• Clear, polite communication
• Transparency and honesty
• Respect for customer property
• Active listening and empathy
• Quick response times
• Consistent follow‑through

1. First Contact Standards

• Answer calls/messages promptly
• Greet customer warmly and professionally
• Gather essential job details
• Avoid rushing or interrupting
• Provide clear next steps
• Confirm contact information

2. Scheduling & Confirmation

• Offer realistic time windows
• Confirm appointment details clearly
• Provide preparation instructions (if needed)
• Send reminders when applicable
• Notify customer immediately of any changes

3. On‑Site Interaction

• Arrive on time
• Greet customer politely
• Walk through job scope
• Ask clarifying questions
• Avoid discussing pricing unless authorized
• Maintain professional tone and body language

4. Communication During Work

• Provide updates when appropriate
• Explain delays honestly
• Avoid technical jargon unless needed
• Keep customer informed of major findings
• Maintain calm, confident communication

5. Handling Concerns or Issues

• Listen fully before responding
• Acknowledge customer feelings
• Avoid defensiveness
• Provide solutions or escalate to management
• Document concerns in job notes
• Follow up after resolution

6. Job Completion Interaction

• Walk customer through final results
• Explain what was done clearly
• Answer questions professionally
• Confirm satisfaction
• Thank customer for their business
• Provide next‑step recommendations (if relevant)

7. Post‑Job Follow‑Up

• Send thank‑you message
• Request review or feedback
• Log any issues for improvement
• Update customer record for future service needs

8. Communication Do’s & Don’ts

Do:
• Stay polite and professional
• Keep communication clear and simple
• Maintain positive tone
• Provide accurate information

Don’t:
• Argue or raise your voice
• Make promises outside scope
• Discuss internal issues
• Ignore customer concerns
