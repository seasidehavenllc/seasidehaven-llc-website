Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
Purpose

These Standard Operating Procedures establish consistent workflows, expectations, and operational standards across all divisions of Seaside Haven LLC. SOPs ensure quality, safety, efficiency, and professionalism in every service we provide.

1. Job Intake & Scheduling

• Receive job request via phone, website, or referral
• Enter job details into scheduling system
• Assign division and team based on service type
• Confirm appointment with customer
• Send pre‑arrival reminders when applicable

2. Pre‑Job Preparation

• Review job notes and scope of work
• Verify tools, materials, and equipment needed
• Check vehicle readiness (fuel, loadout, PPE)
• Contact customer if clarification is needed
• Ensure all team members understand the plan

3. Job Site Arrival

• Arrive on time and greet customer professionally
• Walk the site and confirm scope
• Identify hazards or special considerations
• Protect customer property before beginning work
• Set up tools and materials in an organized manner

4. Work Execution Standards

• Follow division‑specific procedures
• Maintain clean, safe work areas
• Use correct tools and materials
• Communicate progress with customer when appropriate
• Avoid shortcuts or unsafe practices

5. Documentation Requirements

• Take before, during, and after photos
• Upload photos to correct job folder
• Label photos when required
• Record notes for unexpected findings
• Complete digital job report before leaving site

6. Customer Interaction Standards

• Be respectful and professional at all times
• Do not discuss pricing unless authorized
• Do not make promises outside scope
• Direct all concerns to management
• Maintain clear, polite communication

7. Job Completion

• Clean and organize job site
• Verify work meets quality standards
• Walk final results with customer (if applicable)
• Confirm customer satisfaction
• Close job in system and submit documentation

8. Post‑Job Follow‑Up

• Send thank‑you message or follow‑up
• Request review or feedback
• Log any issues for future improvement
• Update customer record for future service needs
