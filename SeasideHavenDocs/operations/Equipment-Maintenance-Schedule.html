Equipment Maintenance Schedule
Purpose

This schedule outlines routine maintenance requirements for tools, vehicles, and equipment used across all divisions of Seaside Haven LLC. Regular maintenance ensures safety, reliability, and long‑term performance.

Daily Maintenance

• Inspect tools for damage
• Clean equipment after use
• Check battery levels and charge tools
• Empty debris from vacuums, blowers, and collection units
• Verify PPE condition
• Inspect vehicle interior for cleanliness
• Report damaged or missing items

Weekly Maintenance

• Deep clean tools and equipment
• Inspect extension cords and hoses
• Sharpen blades (mowers, trimmers, saws)
• Check tire pressure on vehicles and trailers
• Inspect vehicle fluids (oil, coolant, washer fluid)
• Test backup batteries and chargers
• Restock PPE and consumables

Monthly Maintenance

• Full vehicle inspection
• Oil level check and top‑off
• Inspect belts, filters, and spark plugs
• Lubricate moving parts on tools
• Replace worn blades or attachments
• Test generators and backup power units
• Inspect ladders and safety equipment

Quarterly Maintenance

• Full vehicle service (oil change, filter replacement)
• Deep inspection of all division‑specific equipment
• Replace worn tires or wheels
• Pressure test hoses and fittings
• Inspect trailer wiring and lights
• Update maintenance logs
• Review equipment replacement needs

Annual Maintenance

• Comprehensive vehicle service
• Replace major wear components
• Full calibration of specialized tools
• Safety compliance inspection
• Equipment retirement or upgrade planning
• Renew equipment certifications (if applicable)
