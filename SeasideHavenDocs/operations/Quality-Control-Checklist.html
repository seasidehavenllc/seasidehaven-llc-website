Quality Control Checklist
Purpose

This checklist ensures that all work performed by Seaside Haven LLC meets company standards for quality, safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. It is used across all divisions to maintain consistent results.

Pre‑Job Quality Checks

• Job notes reviewed and understood
• Tools and materials verified
• PPE available and in good condition
• Vehicle loadout complete
• Customer contacted if clarification needed
• Weather or site conditions reviewed (if relevant)

On‑Site Quality Checks

• Arrived on time
• Professional greeting and communication
• Scope confirmed with customer
• Hazards identified and addressed
• Customer property protected
• Work area organized and safe
• Correct tools used for each task
• Division‑specific standards followed

Workmanship Standards

• Work performed to industry standards
• No shortcuts taken
• Materials installed or used correctly
• Measurements accurate
• Finishing work clean and consistent
• No visible defects or missed areas
• All tasks completed as described in job notes

Documentation Requirements

• Before photos taken
• Progress photos taken
• After photos taken
• Photos uploaded to correct job folder
• Notes added for unexpected findings
• Digital job report completed before leaving site

Customer Satisfaction

• Customer walked through final results
• Customer questions answered professionally
• Customer confirms satisfaction
• No unresolved concerns
• Follow‑up needs documented
• Customer thanked for their business

Post‑Job Quality Checks

• Work area cleaned
• Tools returned to vehicle
• Materials accounted for
• Vehicle cleaned and organized
• Job closed in system
• Any issues logged for improvement
