Service Dispatch Workflow
Purpose

This workflow outlines the complete process for scheduling, assigning, and dispatching service jobs across all divisions of Seaside Haven LLC. It ensures efficient coordination, clear communication, and consistent customer experience.

1. Job Intake

• Receive job request via phone, website, or referral
• Gather essential details:
– Customer name
– Address
– Contact information
– Service type
– Preferred date/time
– Special notes or hazards
• Enter job into scheduling system
• Assign job category to correct division

2. Scheduling

• Review team availability
• Match job with appropriate contractor or team
• Confirm estimated duration
• Verify required tools and materials
• Contact customer to confirm appointment
• Send confirmation message or email

3. Pre‑Dispatch Preparation

• Review job notes and scope
• Ensure team understands expectations
• Prepare tools, materials, and PPE
• Load vehicle with required equipment
• Check weather conditions (if relevant)
• Notify customer of estimated arrival window

4. Dispatch

• Team departs on time
• Dispatcher updates job status to “In Transit”
• Team contacts customer when en route (if required)
• Dispatcher monitors progress and availability
• Adjust schedule if delays occur

5. On‑Site Workflow

• Arrive and greet customer professionally
• Walk through job scope
• Identify hazards or special considerations
• Protect customer property
• Begin work following SOPs
• Document progress with photos
• Communicate updates when appropriate

6. Job Completion

• Clean and organize job site
• Take final photos
• Walk final results with customer (if applicable)
• Confirm satisfaction
• Update job status to “Completed”
• Submit digital job report

7. Post‑Dispatch Follow‑Up

• Send thank‑you message
• Request review or feedback
• Log any issues or follow‑up needs
• Update customer record
• Prepare team for next assignment
