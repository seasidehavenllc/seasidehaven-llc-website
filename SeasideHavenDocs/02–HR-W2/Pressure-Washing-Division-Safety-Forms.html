Pressure Washing Division Safety Forms
Purpose

These forms must be completed by all W2 employees assigned to the Pressure Washing Division. They confirm the employee understands and agrees to follow all safety rules, PPE requirements, equipment procedures, and hazard awareness specific to pressure washing work.

1. Pressure Washing Safety Agreement

I acknowledge that pressure washing work involves hazards including high‑pressure water streams, chemicals, slippery surfaces, ladders, electrical risks, and property damage potential. I agree to follow all safety rules, supervisor instructions, and PPE requirements at all times.

2. PPE Requirements Acknowledgment

I acknowledge that the following PPE is mandatory for pressure washing work:
• Safety glasses or face shield
• Waterproof gloves
• Non‑slip waterproof boots
• Long pants
• Hearing protection (gas units)
• Respirator when using chemicals

I agree to wear required PPE at all times and understand failure to do so may result in disciplinary action or removal from job sites.
