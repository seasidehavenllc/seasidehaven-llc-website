W2 Employee Agreement
Purpose

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions of employment for W2 employees of Seaside Haven LLC. All employees must review and sign this document prior to beginning work.

1. Employment Status

The employee is hired as a W2 employee of Seaside Haven LLC. All payroll taxes, withholdings, and employer contributions will be handled by the company.

Employment with Seaside Haven LLC is at-will, meaning either the employee or the company may terminate employment at any time, with or without cause.

2. Division Assignment

Upon hiring, the employee is assigned to one specific division based on their skills, experience, and qualifications. This division becomes the employee’s primary work role and daily job function.

The employee’s assigned division is:
Division: ____________________________________________

Employees do not automatically work in other divisions. Cross‑division work requires:
• Completion of that division’s paperwork
• Completion of safety/compliance training
• Licenses or certifications if required
• Approval from management

Employees may request cross‑training, but Seaside Haven LLC reserves the right to approve or deny such requests.

3. Job Duties & Expectations

The employee agrees to perform the duties assigned within their designated division. Duties may vary based on job site, customer needs, and operational requirements.

The employee agrees to:
• Follow all division‑specific procedures
• Maintain professionalism
• Follow instructions from supervisors
• Use company tools and equipment responsibly
• Represent Seaside Haven LLC positively while on duty

4. Work Schedule

Work hours may vary depending on job assignments, weather conditions, and customer scheduling. Employees must arrive on time and be prepared for work at the scheduled start time.

5. Compensation

The employee will be paid hourly wages according to the company’s payroll schedule. Overtime will be paid in accordance with federal and state laws.

Direct deposit is required unless otherwise approved by management.

6. Conduct & Behavior

Employees must maintain professional conduct at all times. The following behaviors are prohibited:
• Harassment or discrimination
• Theft or misuse of company property
• Working under the influence of drugs or alcohol
• Violence or threats
• Excessive absenteeism or tardiness

7. Safety Requirements

Employees must follow all safety rules, including PPE requirements, division‑specific safety procedures, and hazard awareness guidelines.

Failure to follow safety rules may result in disciplinary action or termination.

8. Confidentiality

Employees must protect customer information, company data, pricing, and internal procedures. Sharing confidential information without authorization is prohibited.
