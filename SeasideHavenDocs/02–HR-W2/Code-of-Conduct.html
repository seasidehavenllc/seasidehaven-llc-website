Code of Conduct & Professional Standards
Purpose

This Code of Conduct outlines the professional expectations for all W2 employees of Seaside Haven LLC. These standards ensure a safe, respectful, and productive work environment across all divisions.

1. Professional Behavior

Employees must conduct themselves professionally at all times while representing Seaside Haven LLC. This includes:
• Respecting coworkers, customers, and management
• Using appropriate language
• Maintaining a positive and cooperative attitude
• Following instructions from supervisors
• Avoiding disruptive or hostile behavior

2. Attendance & Punctuality

Employees are expected to arrive on time and ready to work at their scheduled start time. Excessive tardiness or absenteeism may result in disciplinary action.

If an employee will be late or absent, they must notify management as early as possible.

3. Appearance & Dress Code

Employees must maintain a clean, professional appearance appropriate for their assigned division. Required PPE must be worn at all times when applicable.

Clothing must be safe, functional, and free of offensive graphics or language.

4. Customer Interaction

Employees must treat all customers with respect and professionalism. This includes:
• Clear communication
• Honesty and transparency
• Respecting customer property
• Avoiding arguments or confrontations

Any customer issues must be reported to management immediately.

5. Company Property & Equipment

Employees are responsible for the proper use and care of company tools, vehicles, and equipment.

Misuse, negligence, or intentional damage may result in disciplinary action or financial liability.

6. Substance Use

Employees may not work under the influence of alcohol, illegal drugs, or impairing substances. Violations will result in immediate disciplinary action, up to termination.

7. Harassment & Discrimination

Seaside Haven LLC maintains a zero‑tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, or bullying. All employees must treat others with dignity and respect.

8. Confidentiality

Employees must protect customer information, company data, pricing, and internal procedures. Sharing confidential information without authorization is prohibited.

9. Division-Specific Conduct & Safety Compliance

All employees of Seaside Haven LLC are assigned to one specific division at the time of hire. Each division has its own safety rules, PPE requirements, conduct standards, and operational procedures.

Employees must follow all Code of Conduct, PPE, and safety requirements for their assigned division at all times. Division-specific rules may include requirements such as steel toe or composite toe boots, respirators, gloves, eye protection, hearing protection, or other mandatory equipment depending on the division.

Employees are not permitted to perform work in other divisions unless:
• They have completed that division’s paperwork
• They have completed all required compliance and safety training
• They have obtained any required licenses or certifications
• Management has approved the cross-division assignment

Division-specific conduct and PPE requirements will be provided in the employee’s division packet. Employees are responsible for understanding and following all rules for their division.

10. Compliance

Employees must comply with all company policies, safety rules, and division‑specific procedures. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.
