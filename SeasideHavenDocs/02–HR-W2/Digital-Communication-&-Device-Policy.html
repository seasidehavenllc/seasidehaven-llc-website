Digital Communication & Device Policy
Purpose

This policy outlines the expectations for digital communication, device usage, job tracking, and technology conduct for all W2 employees of Seaside Haven LLC. These rules ensure professionalism, accurate job documentation, and safe use of company technology.

1. Clock-In & Job Tracking Requirements

All W2 hourly employees are required to clock in and out using the company’s approved time-tracking system, currently Connecteam. Employees must:
• Clock in at the correct job site
• Clock out when leaving the job
• Ensure GPS tracking is enabled
• Follow all Connecteam instructions provided by management

1099 contractors may use a separate tracking system designated by management. Instructions will be provided to contractors upon onboarding.

2. Job Photos & Upload Requirements

Certain divisions require employees to take photos and upload them as part of job documentation. Required uploads may include:
• Before-and-after photos
• Progress photos
• Damage documentation
• Completed work verification

Photos must be:
• Clear and professional
• Accurate representations of the job
• Uploaded to the correct job folder or platform

The company may use platforms such as Connecteam, AccuLynx, or other approved apps for photo uploads and job documentation. Employees must follow the upload requirements for their assigned division.

3. Professional Communication

All digital communication must be professional, respectful, and appropriate. This includes:
• Text messages
• Phone calls
• Emails
• Job-related apps
• Internal messaging platforms

Employees must communicate clearly, avoid inappropriate language, and respond promptly during work hours.

4. Use of Personal Devices

Personal devices may be used for work only when authorized. Employees must:
• Keep personal phone use to a minimum
• Avoid distractions during work
• Never record customers or job sites without permission
• Never store company data on personal devices

Unauthorized photos, videos, or recordings are strictly prohibited.

5. Use of Company Devices

Employees using company devices must:
• Use them only for work-related tasks
• Keep devices clean and protected
• Report damage or malfunction immediately
• Not install unauthorized apps or software
• Not share login credentials

Company devices may not be used for personal entertainment, social media, or non-work browsing.

6. Social Media Conduct

Employees may not post job-related content, customer information, or company details on social media without written permission from management.

Employees must not:
• Discuss internal issues publicly
• Post photos of job sites without approval
• Share customer names or addresses
• Represent themselves as company spokespeople

7. Data Protection

Employees must protect company and customer data by:
• Keeping documents secure
• Not leaving devices unattended
• Not sharing passwords
• Reporting lost or stolen devices immediately

Employees may only access data necessary for their assigned division.

8. Prohibited Digital Actions

The following actions are strictly prohibited:
• Accessing inappropriate websites
• Using devices while operating machinery or driving
• Sharing confidential information
• Using personal accounts for company business
• Downloading unauthorized files
