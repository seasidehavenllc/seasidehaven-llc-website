Operating Agreement
Multi-Member Limited Liability Company

This Operating Agreement is made and entered into by the Members of Seaside Haven LLC, a Virginia Limited Liability Company.

Article 1 – Company Formation

Seaside Haven LLC (“the Company”) was formed as a Limited Liability Company under the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia. The Articles of Organization have been filed with the Virginia State Corporation Commission.

Article 2 – Members

The Members and legal owners of the Company are:
• Dakota Taylor
• Kaylyn Burrola

Article 3 – Purpose

The Company is organized to engage in lawful business activities including, but not limited to:
• Remodeling services
• Landscaping and lawn care services
• Rental property management
• Pressure washing services
• Junk removal services
• Any future divisions added by the Members

Article 4 – Management

The Company is managed jointly by the Members.
Both Members serve as Operators/Administrators of Seaside Haven LLC.

Operators / Administrators:
• Dakota Taylor
• Kaylyn Burrola

Operator Responsibilities:
• Oversee daily operations across all divisions
• Manage scheduling, customer relations, and administrative tasks
• Maintain compliance and documentation standards
• Approve internal and public document releases
• Authorize financial and operational decisions
• Ensure all divisions follow company policies and safety requirements

Both Operators have equal authority in all operational matters.

Article 5 – Liability Protection

Members shall not be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company.

Article 6 – Capital Contributions

Members may contribute capital as needed. No minimum contribution is required.

Article 7 – Profits and Losses

Profits and losses shall be allocated equally unless amended in writing.

Article 8 – Records and Documentation

The Company shall maintain all official business records, documents, contracts, compliance files, and division-specific materials inside the Seaside Haven LLC Document System located at:

www.seasidehavenllc.com

The Document System is divided into two controlled environments:

1. Internal Document System (Private)
• Accessible only by the Owners: Dakota Taylor and Kaylyn Burrola
• Contains all legal documents, compliance records, HR files, financial records, division documents, and operational materials
• Protected by administrative access controls
• Not accessible to the public
• All sensitive documents are locked and require owner-level authorization to view, modify, or export

2. Public Document System (Public-Facing)
• Contains documents intended for customers, vendors, partners, and the general public
• Includes service descriptions, public policies, pricing sheets, marketing materials, and non-sensitive forms
• Public documents are intentionally separated from internal records to protect confidentiality and operational integrity

The Company shall ensure:
• All internal documents remain secured and encrypted
• Public documents are accessible without exposing internal systems
• Only Owners may authorize changes to document access levels
• All records are stored in accordance with Virginia state requirements and industry best practices

Article 9 – Amendments

This Operating Agreement may be amended only with the consent of both Members.
