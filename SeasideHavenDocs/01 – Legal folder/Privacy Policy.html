1. Information We Collect

Seaside Haven LLC may collect the following information:

Personal Information:
• Name
• Address
• Phone number
• Email address

Service Information:
• Service location
• Photos of service areas
• Notes related to service performance
• Division-specific requirements

Payment Information:
• Payment method
• Transaction details
• Billing information

Technical Information:
• Communication logs
• Scheduling records
• Digital forms submitted by clients

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used for:
• Scheduling and completing services
• Customer communication
• Billing and payment processing
• Safety and compliance documentation
• Internal record-keeping
• Service quality improvement
• Legal or regulatory requirements

3. How We Store and Protect Information

All client information is stored in the Seaside Haven LLC internal document system at:

www.seasidehavenllc.com

Security Measures:
• Encrypted storage
• Owner-only access (Dakota & Kaylyn)
• Password vault protection
• No third-party data sharing
• No storage on personal devices
• Secure backups in the Secure Records Index

4. Information Sharing

Seaside Haven LLC does not sell or share client information.

Information may only be shared:
• When legally required
• To comply with a court order
• To complete a service (e.g., subcontractor assistance)
• With explicit client permission

5. Photography & Documentation

Photos or videos may be taken for:
• Liability protection
• Before/after documentation
• Quality assurance
• Compliance records

These images are stored internally and never shared publicly without permission.

6. Client Rights

Clients may request:
• Copies of their records
• Correction of inaccurate information
• Deletion of non-essential data
• Clarification of how their data is used

Requests must be submitted to:
admin@seasidehavenllc.com

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed.
All updates will be posted publicly and stored internally.
