Document Naming Standards
Format

[Division] – [Document Type] – [Title] – [Date]

Examples

Remodeling – Contract – Kitchen Renovation Agreement – 2026-07-14
Landscaping – Checklist – Weekly Lawn Maintenance – 2026-07-14
Pressure Washing – Waiver – Chemical Disclosure – 2026-07-14

Rules

• Use YYYY-MM-DD format for dates.
• Avoid special characters.
• Keep names clear and descriptive.
• Store documents in their correct division folders.
