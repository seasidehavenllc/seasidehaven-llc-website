Seaside Haven LLC Internal Communication Policy Purpose: To establish clear communication standards across all divisions of Seaside Haven LLC. Approved Communication Channels: • Company Email (Primary) • Connecteam (Scheduling, tasks, internal messaging) • Business Phone Line • Official company documents stored in SeasideHavenDocs Communication Standards: • All business communication must remain professional and documented. • Division leads must report weekly updates to Administration. • Customer communication must be logged when relevant to service delivery or disputes. Document Storage: • All official documents must be stored in SeasideHavenDocs. • No division may store documents on personal devices or personal cloud accounts.
