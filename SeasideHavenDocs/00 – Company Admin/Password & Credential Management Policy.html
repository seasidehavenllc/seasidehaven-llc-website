Password & Credential Management Policy
Approved Password Managers

• Bitwarden (Recommended)
• 1Password (Alternative)

Requirements

• All business-related login credentials must be stored in the approved password manager.
• No passwords may be stored in plain text or unsecured notes.
• Access to the Admin Vault is restricted to the Owners and designated administrative personnel.

Vault Categories

• Banking & Finance
• Email & Communication
• Website & Hosting
• Connecteam
• Square / Payment Processing
• Accounting Software
• Division-Specific Tools

Backup Requirements

• Enable encrypted cloud backup.
• Export encrypted vault monthly and store in the “01 – Legal” folder under “Secure Records.”
