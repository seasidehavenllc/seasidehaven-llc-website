Junk Removal Division Safety Forms
Purpose

These forms must be completed by all W2 employees assigned to the Junk Removal Division. They confirm the employee understands and agrees to follow all safety rules, PPE requirements, lifting procedures, equipment rules, and hazard awareness specific to junk removal work.

1. Junk Removal Safety Agreement

I acknowledge that junk removal work involves hazards including heavy lifting, sharp objects, debris, chemicals, mold, insects, rodents, and unsafe structures. I agree to follow all safety rules, supervisor instructions, and PPE requirements at all times.

2. PPE Requirements Acknowledgment

I acknowledge that the following PPE is mandatory for junk removal work:
• Safety glasses
• Heavy‑duty work gloves
• Steel/composite toe boots
• Long pants
• Respirator when required (dust, mold, chemicals)
• Hard hat (when required)

I agree to wear required PPE at all times and understand failure to do so may result in disciplinary action or removal from job sites.

3. Lifting & Handling Safety Acknowledgment

I acknowledge that junk removal work requires safe lifting techniques. I agree to:
• Lift with legs, not back
• Ask for help with heavy items
• Use dollies, straps, or carts when available
• Avoid lifting items beyond my capability
• Report unsafe items immediately

4. Hazard Awareness Acknowledgment

I acknowledge that junk removal work may involve:
• Mold or mildew
• Sharp metal or broken glass
• Chemicals or unknown substances
• Rodents, insects, or wildlife
• Unstable piles or collapsing debris
• Unsafe flooring or structures

I agree to report hazards immediately and stop work if unsafe conditions are present.
