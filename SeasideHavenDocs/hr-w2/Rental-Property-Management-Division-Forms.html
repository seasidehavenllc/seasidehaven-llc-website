Rental Property Management Division Forms
Purpose

These forms must be completed by all W2 employees assigned to the Rental Property Management Division. They confirm the employee understands and agrees to follow all safety rules, PPE requirements, property access rules, tenant interaction guidelines, and hazard awareness specific to rental property management work.

1. Rental Property Safety Agreement

I acknowledge that rental property management work involves hazards including entering occupied or vacant units, unknown conditions, structural issues, pests, cleaning chemicals, and potential tenant interactions. I agree to follow all safety rules, supervisor instructions, and PPE requirements at all times.
