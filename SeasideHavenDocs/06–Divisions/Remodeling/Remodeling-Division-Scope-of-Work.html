Remodeling Division Scope of Work & Task List
Purpose

This document defines the approved scope of work, authorized tasks, and job responsibilities for employees assigned to the Remodeling Division of Seaside Haven LLC. Employees must follow all division-specific procedures and may only perform tasks they are trained and authorized to complete.

Authorized Remodeling Tasks

Remodeling employees are authorized to perform the following tasks when trained and directed by management:

Demolition:
• Removal of drywall
• Removal of flooring
• Removal of cabinets
• Removal of trim, doors, and framing
• Safe disposal of debris

Framing & Carpentry:
• Stud wall installation
• Blocking and reinforcement
• Door and window framing
• Subfloor repair or replacement

Drywall:
• Hanging drywall
• Taping and mudding
• Sanding and finishing

Flooring:
• Vinyl plank installation
• Laminate installation
• Tile installation (if trained)
• Subfloor preparation

Painting:
• Interior painting
• Trim painting
• Surface preparation

Trim & Finish Work:
• Baseboards
• Door casings
• Window trim
• Crown molding (if trained)

Cabinet & Fixture Installation:
• Cabinet hanging
• Hardware installation
• Vanity installation
• Light fixture replacement (non-electrical disconnect)

Tasks Requiring Special Authorization

The following tasks may only be performed by employees who have received specific training and written approval from management:

Electrical Work:
• Only licensed electricians or approved personnel may perform electrical tasks
• Employees may NOT disconnect live wiring

Plumbing Work:
• Only licensed plumbers or approved personnel may perform plumbing tasks
• Employees may NOT modify water lines or drains

Structural Modifications:
• Load-bearing wall removal
• Beam installation
• Foundation or structural repairs

Tile Work:
• Requires division approval and skill verification

Prohibited Tasks

Remodeling employees are NOT permitted to perform the following tasks under any circumstances:


• Live electrical work
• Plumbing modifications
• HVAC repairs or installation
• Roofing
• Asbestos removal
• Mold remediation without certification
• Pest control
• Any task outside their assigned division

Performing prohibited tasks may result in disciplinary action or termination.

Job Documentation Requirements

Remodeling employees must upload:
• Before photos
• Progress photos
• After photos
• Any unexpected findings or damage

All photos must be uploaded to Connecteam, AccuLynx, or another approved platform.

Workflow Expectations

Employees must:
• Follow supervisor instructions
• Complete tasks in the correct order
• Maintain a clean job site
• Communicate issues immediately
• Follow all safety and PPE rules
