Remodeling Division Job Completion Checklist
Purpose

This checklist ensures all remodeling work is completed to company standards before employees leave the job site. Every item must be reviewed and confirmed to maintain quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

1. Work Area Completion

Employees must verify the following:
• All assigned tasks for the day are fully completed
• No tools or materials are left behind
• All cuts, installs, and finishes meet company quality standards
• No exposed hazards remain
• All fixtures, cabinets, trim, or flooring are properly secured

2. Cleanliness & Job Site Reset

Before leaving the job site, employees must:
• Sweep and clean the work area
• Remove debris and trash
• Organize tools and materials
• Protect any unfinished areas
• Ensure customer property is left clean and respected

A clean job site is required at the end of every shift.

3. Photo Documentation

Employees must upload the following photos:
• Before photos (if not already uploaded)
• Progress photos
• After photos
• Any damage, hazards, or unexpected findings

Photos must be:
• Clear and well-lit
• Taken from proper angles
• Uploaded to the correct job folder
• Labeled correctly when required

Uploads must be completed through Connecteam, AccuLynx, or another approved platform.

4. Communication & Reporting

Before leaving the job site, employees must:
• Notify the supervisor of job status
• Report any issues, delays, or material shortages
• Document any hazards or structural concerns
• Confirm next steps or return schedule

Employees must follow all digital communication rules for their division.

5. Tool & Equipment Check

Employees must:
• Account for all tools
• Clean tools before storing
• Report damaged or malfunctioning equipment
• Secure tools in the designated vehicle or storage area

6. Safety Verification

Employees must confirm:
• No hazards are left behind
• All demo debris is removed or contained
• All sharp objects are disposed of properly
• All PPE was used correctly throughout the shift

Safety violations must be reported immediately.

7. Final Walkthrough

Before clocking out, employees must:
• Walk the entire work area
• Verify all tasks are complete
• Ensure the site is clean and safe
• Confirm no tools or materials are left behind
