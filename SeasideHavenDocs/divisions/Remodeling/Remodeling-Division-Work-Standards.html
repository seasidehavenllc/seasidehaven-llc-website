Remodeling Division Work Standards & Procedures
Purpose

This document outlines the work standards, operational procedures, and professional expectations for employees assigned to the Remodeling Division of Seaside Haven LLC. Remodeling work requires precision, professionalism, and strict adherence to company workflow.

Job Site Arrival & Setup

Remodeling employees must:
• Arrive on time and clock in using Connecteam
• Review job notes, scope of work, and supervisor instructions
• Walk the job site to identify hazards or structural concerns
• Set up tools, PPE, and materials in an organized manner
• Protect customer property before beginning work

Employees must not begin demolition or construction until the supervisor confirms the plan.

Work Quality Standards

Remodeling work must meet the company’s quality expectations. Employees must:
• Follow measurements precisely
• Use correct materials and fasteners
• Maintain clean cuts, straight lines, and proper alignment
• Follow manufacturer installation guidelines
• Avoid rushing or cutting corners

Any mistakes must be reported immediately so they can be corrected properly.

Communication & Coordination

Remodeling employees must maintain clear communication with:
• Supervisors
• Team members
• Customers (when authorized)

Employees must:
• Ask questions when unsure
• Report issues immediately
• Follow all digital communication rules
• Use Connecteam or AccuLynx for job updates when required

Job Documentation Requirements

Remodeling employees must upload:
• Before photos
• Progress photos
• After photos
• Any damage or unexpected findings

Photos must be:
• Clear and well-lit
• Taken from proper angles
• Uploaded to the correct job folder
• Labeled correctly when required

Documentation is mandatory for all remodeling jobs.

Cleanliness & Job Site Management

Remodeling employees must:
• Keep the job site clean throughout the day
• Remove debris regularly
• Organize tools and materials
• Protect floors, walls, and customer belongings
• Leave the site clean at the end of each shift

A clean job site is required for safety and professionalism.

Customer Interaction Rules

Remodeling employees must:
• Be respectful and professional
• Avoid discussing pricing or estimates
• Avoid making promises or guarantees
• Direct all customer questions to management

Employees may not enter areas of the home not related to the job.

Material Handling & Storage

Employees must:
• Handle materials carefully
• Store materials safely and neatly
• Avoid damaging customer property
• Verify measurements before cutting

Waste materials must be disposed of properly.

Division-Specific Conduct

Remodeling employees must follow:
• All remodeling safety rules
• All PPE requirements
• All digital communication rules
• All division-specific procedures

Failure to follow division standards may result in disciplinary action.
