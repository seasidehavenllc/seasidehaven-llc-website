Remodeling Division – Job Completion Checklist
Purpose

This Job Completion Checklist ensures that all remodeling work performed under the Seaside Haven LLC Remodeling Division meets required standards for safety, quality, and compliance. Contractors and employees must complete this checklist at the end of every remodeling project.

Safety Verification

• All PPE was worn throughout the project
• No safety violations occurred
• All hazardous materials were handled properly
• Tools and equipment were used safely
• No injuries or incidents occurred
• Any issues were reported immediately

Work Quality Verification

• Work meets professional remodeling standards
• All tasks listed in the Work Order were completed
• All installations are secure and structurally sound
• All measurements and cuts are accurate
• All finishing work is clean and consistent
• No defects or deficiencies remain

Site Cleanup

• All debris removed from the jobsite
• All tools and materials accounted for
• Floors, surfaces, and work areas cleaned
• Customer property left in good condition
• No damage to customer property
• All waste disposed of properly

Customer Communication

• Customer notified of job completion
• Customer questions answered professionally
• Customer informed of any follow‑up work (if applicable)
• Customer satisfied with the final result
• Photos taken for documentation (if required)

Final Verification

• All required documentation submitted
• Work Order marked as completed
• Supervisor notified of completion
• No outstanding issues remain
• Project ready for final approval
