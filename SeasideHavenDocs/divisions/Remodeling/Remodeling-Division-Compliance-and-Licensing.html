Remodeling Division – Compliance & Licensing Requirements
Purpose

This document outlines the compliance, licensing, and regulatory requirements for all individuals performing remodeling work under the Seaside Haven LLC Remodeling Division. These standards ensure legal operation, safety, and professional quality across all remodeling projects.

Licensing Requirements

• All contractors must comply with Virginia state remodeling and construction regulations.
• Contractors must maintain any required trade licenses or certifications.
• Contractors must provide proof of licensing upon onboarding.
• Contractors must notify Seaside Haven LLC of any changes, suspensions, or expirations.
• Specialty work (electrical, plumbing, HVAC) requires appropriate state‑issued licenses.

Regulatory Compliance

• All remodeling work must comply with Virginia building codes.
• Contractors must follow local county and city permit requirements.
• Work requiring permits must not begin until permits are approved.
• Contractors must follow OSHA safety standards.
• Contractors must comply with environmental and disposal regulations.

Jobsite Compliance

• PPE must be worn at all times on active remodeling sites.
• Contractors must maintain clean and safe work areas.
• Tools and equipment must be in safe working condition.
• Hazardous materials must be handled according to regulations.
• Any violations must be reported immediately.

Quality & Work Standards

• All remodeling work must meet professional industry standards.
• Contractors must follow instructions listed in each Work Order.
• Any deficiencies must be corrected promptly.
• Contractors must ensure structural integrity and safe installation practices.
• Contractors must maintain respectful conduct in customer homes and businesses.

Insurance Requirements

• Contractors must maintain active liability insurance.
• Proof of insurance must be submitted before beginning work.
• Coverage must remain active throughout all remodeling projects.
• Contractors must notify Seaside Haven LLC of any changes in coverage.
