Remodeling Division – Scope of Work
Purpose

This Scope of Work defines the types of remodeling services performed under the Seaside Haven LLC Remodeling Division. It outlines the responsibilities, job categories, and service expectations for all remodeling projects.

General Remodeling Services

• Interior renovations
• Exterior renovations
• Structural modifications (non‑load‑bearing unless permitted)
• Flooring installation and replacement
• Drywall installation, repair, and finishing
• Painting and surface finishing
• Trim, molding, and carpentry work
• Fixture installation (non‑licensed categories)

Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

• Cabinet installation and modification
• Countertop installation
• Tile installation and repair
• Backsplash installation
• Non‑licensed plumbing fixture replacement
• Vanity and hardware installation

Structural & Framing Work

• Wall framing (non‑load‑bearing)
• Doorway modifications
• Window framing adjustments
• Subfloor repair and replacement
• Minor structural reinforcement (as permitted)

Licensed Work Limitations

The Remodeling Division does NOT perform the following unless a licensed subcontractor is assigned:
• Electrical work requiring a state license
• Plumbing work requiring a state license
• HVAC installation or repair
• Major structural modifications requiring engineering approval
• Any work requiring specialized permits beyond general remodeling

Customer Expectations

• Professional communication throughout the project
• Respect for customer property and privacy
• Clean and organized work areas
• Accurate timelines and updates
• High‑quality workmanship
• Compliance with all safety and building regulations

Division Responsibilities

• Provide clear Work Orders for each project
• Ensure compliance with Virginia building codes
• Maintain safety standards on all job sites
• Assign qualified personnel to each project
• Verify completion and quality of all remodeling work
