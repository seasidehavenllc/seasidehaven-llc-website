Remote Online Notary Procedure
Purpose

This procedure outlines the steps required to complete Remote Online Notarization (RON) under Seaside Haven LLC. These guidelines ensure compliance with Virginia RON laws and maintain secure, verifiable digital notarization practices.

Requirements

• Notary must hold an active Virginia RON commission
• Signer must have a device capable of audio/video communication
• Signer must present valid government‑issued photo identification
• Document must be provided in electronic format
• Session must be recorded and securely stored
• Identity verification must follow credential analysis and knowledge‑based authentication (KBA)

Step 1 – Scheduling the RON Session

• Signer submits a notarization request
• Notary confirms eligibility and document type
• Notary schedules a secure online session
• Signer receives instructions and meeting link

Step 2 – Pre‑Session Verification

• Signer uploads document for review
• Notary checks document for completeness
• Signer completes identity verification:
– Credential analysis
– Knowledge‑based authentication (KBA)
• Notary confirms signer identity before proceeding

Step 3 – Live Audio/Video Session

• Notary starts secure recording
• Signer appears on camera and presents ID
• Notary confirms identity matches ID
• Signer verbally acknowledges understanding of the document
• Signer electronically signs the document
• Notary completes electronic notarial certificate
• Notary applies electronic seal and signature

Step 4 – Post‑Session Actions

• Notary saves audio/video recording
• Notary logs notarization in the Notary Log
• Notary stores electronic journal entry securely
• Final notarized document is sent to signer
• Notary retains records according to Virginia retention laws

Security Requirements

• All sessions must use encrypted communication
• Electronic seals must meet state security standards
• Recordings must be stored in secure, backed‑up systems
• Access to RON records must be restricted to authorized personnel
• Any suspected fraud must be reported immediately

Prohibited Actions

• Notarizing without proper identity verification
• Using unapproved platforms or unsecured communication tools
• Notarizing incomplete or blank documents
• Notarizing for individuals who fail KBA or credential analysis
• Providing legal advice or interpreting document contents
