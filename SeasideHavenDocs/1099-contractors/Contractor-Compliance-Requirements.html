Contractor Compliance Requirements
Purpose

This document outlines the compliance requirements that all independent contractors must follow while performing work for Seaside Haven LLC. These standards ensure safety, legal compliance, and consistent service quality across all divisions.

Legal & Regulatory Compliance

• Contractor must comply with all federal, state, and local laws.
• Contractor must maintain any required trade licenses or certifications.
• Contractor must follow OSHA safety standards and division-specific rules.
• Contractor must report any violations or hazards immediately.

Insurance Requirements

• Contractor must maintain active liability insurance.
• Contractor must provide proof of insurance upon onboarding.
• Contractor must notify Seaside Haven LLC of any changes in coverage.
• Contractor must maintain coverage appropriate for the work performed.

Safety & Conduct

• Contractor must follow all PPE requirements.
• Contractor must follow division-specific safety rules.
• Contractor must maintain professional conduct on all job sites.
• Contractor must report incidents or injuries immediately.
• Contractor must not engage in unsafe or reckless behavior.

Work Quality Standards

• Contractor must complete work to professional standards.
• Contractor must follow instructions listed in each Work Order.
• Contractor must correct any deficiencies identified by supervisors.
• Contractor must maintain clean and organized work areas.

Equipment & Tools

• Contractor is responsible for providing their own tools unless otherwise agreed.
• Contractor must ensure tools are safe and in working condition.
• Contractor must follow equipment checkout procedures when using company-owned tools.

Communication Requirements

• Contractor must maintain reliable communication with supervisors.
• Contractor must respond promptly to job assignments.
• Contractor must notify supervisors of delays or issues.
• Contractor must maintain professional communication with clients.
