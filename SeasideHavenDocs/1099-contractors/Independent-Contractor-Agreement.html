Independent Contractor Agreement
Purpose

This Independent Contractor Agreement outlines the terms under which the Contractor will provide services to Seaside Haven LLC. This agreement establishes responsibilities, payment terms, and compliance requirements.

Contractor Status

• Contractor is not an employee of Seaside Haven LLC.
• Contractor is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and licensing.
• Contractor determines their own methods, tools, and work processes unless otherwise agreed.
• Contractor is not eligible for employee benefits.

Scope of Work

• Contractor will perform services as assigned by Seaside Haven LLC.
• Work orders will define specific tasks, deadlines, and expectations.
• Contractor agrees to complete all work to professional standards.

Payment Terms

• Contractor will be paid according to the terms listed in each Work Order.
• Payments are issued upon completion and approval of work.
• Contractor must submit invoices or payment authorization forms when required.

Compliance Requirements

• Contractor must follow all safety rules and division standards.
• Contractor must comply with all federal, state, and local regulations.
• Contractor must maintain required insurance coverage.
• Contractor must report incidents immediately.

Confidentiality

• Contractor agrees not to disclose company information.
• Contractor agrees to protect customer data and internal documents.
• A separate Non‑Disclosure Agreement may be required.

Termination

• Either party may terminate this agreement with written notice.
• Seaside Haven LLC may terminate immediately for safety violations, misconduct, or fraud.
• Contractor must return all company property upon termination.

Acknowledgment & Signature
