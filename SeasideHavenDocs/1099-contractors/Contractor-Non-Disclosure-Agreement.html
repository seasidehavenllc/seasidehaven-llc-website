Contractor Non‑Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
Purpose

This Non‑Disclosure Agreement (NDA) outlines the confidentiality obligations of contractors performing work for Seaside Haven LLC. Contractors may be exposed to sensitive business information, customer data, internal documents, or proprietary processes. This agreement ensures that all such information remains protected.

Confidential Information

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to:
• Customer names, addresses, and contact information
• Work orders, job details, and project specifications
• Internal business processes and operational methods
• Pricing, financial information, and payment records
• Employee or contractor personal information
• Any non‑public information related to Seaside Haven LLC

Contractor Obligations

• Contractor agrees not to disclose confidential information to any third party.
• Contractor agrees not to use confidential information for personal gain.
• Contractor agrees to protect all documents, digital files, and communications.
• Contractor agrees to return or destroy confidential materials upon request.
• Contractor agrees to report any data breach or unauthorized access immediately.

Permitted Disclosure

Contractor may disclose confidential information only when:
• Required by law or court order
• Authorized in writing by Seaside Haven LLC
• Necessary to complete assigned work, and only to approved parties

Term of Agreement

• This NDA remains in effect during and after the contractor’s relationship with Seaside Haven LLC.
• Confidentiality obligations continue indefinitely unless formally released in writing.

Violations

• Any breach of this agreement may result in termination of contracted work.
• Seaside Haven LLC may pursue legal action for damages caused by unauthorized disclosure.
• Contractor may be held financially responsible for losses caused by a breach.
