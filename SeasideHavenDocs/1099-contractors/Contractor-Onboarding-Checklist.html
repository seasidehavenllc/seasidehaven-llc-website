Contractor Onboarding Checklist
Purpose

This checklist ensures that all independent contractors working with Seaside Haven LLC complete required onboarding steps before beginning any work. Contractors must review and acknowledge all listed items.

Required Documentation

• Valid government-issued ID
• Proof of insurance coverage
• Completed Independent Contractor Agreement
• Completed Contractor Compliance Requirements form
• Completed Contractor Insurance Verification form

Safety & Compliance

• Review division-specific safety rules
• Review PPE requirements
• Review incident reporting procedures
• Review jobsite conduct expectations
• Review emergency contact procedures

Tools & Equipment

• Contractor confirms they will provide their own tools unless otherwise agreed
• Contractor verifies tools are in safe working condition
• Contractor understands equipment checkout rules (if applicable)

Work Order Process

• Contractor understands how work orders are assigned
• Contractor understands how to submit completed work
• Contractor understands approval and payment timelines
• Contractor understands communication expectations with supervisors

Payment & Invoicing

• Contractor understands payment authorization requirements
• Contractor understands invoice submission procedures
• Contractor understands payment schedule
• Contractor understands documentation required for payment release
